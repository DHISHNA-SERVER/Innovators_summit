About Innovator Summit

Learn, Share, Innovate, Network And Collaborate

 This is an All-Kerala summit for involving different communities and professional students across Kerala aimed to upskill their talents and find their potential. It's a conference that is going to bring together various business owners, change-makers, thought leaders, and entrepreneurs to discuss, challenge, and learn about the current ideas regarding innovation. It will be bringing together various innovators who discovered something new in their respective fields, and students for learning about various ideas and innovations that are shaping the world now a days. The event has been designed to provide an innovative and comprehensive overview of latest developments. We are excited about the outcome and hope that you will find this summit fruitful, enjoyable and worthwhile.

What we offer

Hands on workshops

The summit offers workshops on various domains including the latest and most exciting technologies which are set to be pioneer fields in tomorrow’s world.These workshops are an integral part of skillset enhancement.

Talks by professional speakers

The summit brings about talks and seminars regarding innovations and contemporary ideas by various thought leaders, entrepreneurs and change-makers who have discovered something new in their respective fields.

Mentorship

A great mentor can not only shape a great and fulfilling career, but could also help in building a remarkable personality. The summit helps in connecting with various experienced innovators and entrepreneurs, who could guide students, to improve, not only their competency in the professional world, but also their skillsets and personality overall.

Networking with Communities

The summit brings together innovators from all around Kerala irrespective of their professional fields, summoning various communities and providing an opportunity for students to nurture a great network with various communities. Sharing each others perspective on different subjects is a great way to network and bring people together.

F.A.Q

  • What is the aim of Innovator's summit?

    Innovator's Summit is aimed at providing a platform to student developers and professionals to bridge the gap between academics and industry with hands on sessions and workshops that help students and professionals leverage their practical knowledge. We aim to create opportunities for tech enthusiasts to learn from the best speakers, stalwarts in their respective fields, and gain an insight into different leading edge technologies

  • Who is conducting this event?

    The Innovator's summit is an joint venture by all the communities in kerala and Cochin University of Science and Technology.

  • Will lunch be provided?

    Yes, lunch will be provided to all.

  • Will certificates be provided?

    Yes, certificates will be provided to all. There's also a ton of goodies up for grab.

  • Can I present a topic in the Community Day?

    Yes, you are welcome to come forward and present topics. Please contact us and we will allocate a slot for you.

  • Is it free, how to register for the event?

    This is not a free event . We will be charging a minimum amount from the attendees.

  • Can I cancel my registration?

    Cancellation is not allowed, we will not be processing any refunds. However, you can transfer the ticket to someone else. Contact us if you would like to transfer your ticket to someone else in case if you are notable to attend the event.

  • What do you mean by tracks?

    The workshop in the afternoon will be having two parallel running sessions. Track 1 is about the topic Power of Serverless with AWS Amplify, Track 2 is UI/UX designing. Participants can choose either of the Tracks, they can’t choose both.

  • Do we have walk-in registration?

    No, all attendees should register in advance. Tickets will be closed by the second week of January based on availability.

  • Are there any tracks in Tech-talks?

    Tech-talks doesn't have any tracks. It is a single track session with the speakers from Google, AWS, Microsoft UG, Wordpress.com.

  • Additional questions?

    We are always happy to hear from you. You can reach us for sponsorship enquires, topic proposals, ticketing, feedback or any general question on Innovators Summit, please drop an email to summit@dhishna.org
